IMPORTANT: Replace `YOUR_STRIPE_CHECKOUT_LINK_HERE` in `index.html` with your actual Stripe Payment Link for $9.99. You will also need to manually collect the `landingPageUrl` and `questions` from the form submission to fulfill the service. Stripe's prebuilt checkout does not directly pass custom form data without client-side JavaScript or a backend. For this MVP, you might need to combine a simple Stripe Payment Link with a post-purchase email instructing the user to reply with their URL/questions, or use a more advanced integration.
